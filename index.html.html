FAQ

Questions

Do I need a team to apply?
No. You can apply solo and teams form on the first evening around the ideas pitched. Many of the best teams at previous editions met for the first time at the event. If you do have a team, that's fine too — each member still needs to apply individually.
Do I need to have an idea?
No. If you have an idea, bring it and pitch it on day one. If you don't, you'll join a team working on an idea you find compelling. What matters is that you're ready to build.
Is it really free?
Yes. Entry is completely free. Food, coffee, and the workspace are all covered by our partners. All we ask is that you show up ready to build for 48 hours.
Who owns the IP?
You do. 100%. Everything built at Hack48 belongs to the team who built it. We don't take equity, rights, or claims over anything created at the event. Period.
What should I bring?
Your laptop, chargers, and any hardware you need. We'll handle food, coffee, workspace, and wifi. Some teams bring sleeping bags — it's up to you whether you power through or get a full night's rest (most successful teams sleep).
What kind of projects can I build?
Anything. Software, apps, hardware, AI products, consumer tools, B2B SaaS — there are no restrictions on what you build. The best ideas solve a real problem and have a viable path to a business.
Can I apply to both cities?
Yes. If you're keen on both Auckland and Sydney, apply to both. We'll confirm spots closer to the event date.
